Talks

The Conference on Quantum Information Processing (QIP, 5 talks) is the premier and the most competitive conference in quantum information science. The Conference on the Theory of Quantum Computation, Communication and Cryptography (TQC, 3 talks) and the Asian Quantum Information Science Conference (AQIS, 1 long talk + 7 short talks) are the leading conferences in the field. The IEEE International Symposium on Information Theory (ISIT, 3 talks) is the main event in information theory. The International Conference on Continuous Optimization (ICCOPT, 1 talk) is a flagship conference of the Mathematical Optimization Society.

In the following list, [*] indicates delivery by my co-author.
