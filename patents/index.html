Patents

Since 2021, I have filed 63 patent applications, 38 of which have already been granted. These patents address the engineering challenges of quantum technology and cover a range of important areas such as quantum data structures, quantum circuit compilation, classical simulation of quantum algorithms, quantum network architectures, quantum hardware interfaces, measurement-based quantum computation, distributed quantum computing. The majority of these patents have been integrated into the Baidu Quantum Platform, which is a full-stack quantum software and hardware platform developed by Baidu.
